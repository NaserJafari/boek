THE GAME BOOK

You’re playing Tic Tac Toe

The game starts by tapping one of the boxes.

The first player will put down their icon, and then it’s the second player's turn to click on one of the boxes to put down their own icon.

This goes on until someone gets 3 of their icons in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) or if the entire board is filled up without any clear winner, which will turn the game into a draw. If the game becomes a draw, you can click the reset button to play again until you win, same with if you lose.
